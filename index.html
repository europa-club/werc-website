1. Overview

⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER: WERC is not a stablecoin, is not pegged 1:1 to EUR, and Euro Finance is not affiliated with Circle or Euro Coin (EURC). The price of WERC is fully market-driven.

Euro Finance (WERC) is a euro-themed, multi-chain DeFi asset deployed on Arbitrum One, BNB Smart Chain, and TRON. The token is designed for transfers, liquidity provision, and other DeFi use cases, while explicitly avoiding any representation as an official or collateralized EUR stablecoin.

Key Principle: We are an independent entity. We do not rely on fiat backing, algorithmic pegs, or centralized issuers. WERC derives its value purely from market demand and utility within the DeFi ecosystem.

Primary Network: Arbitrum One (ERC-20) at 0x17d805a3f817e0e4E4d46c59d7c155bD907D8333 is the official primary contract.

5. Utility and Use Cases

  • Transfers: Fast, low-cost transfers of euro-themed value between supported chains
  • Liquidity Provision: Use in liquidity pools and automated market makers on supported DEXes
  • DeFi Integrations: Farming, lending, staking, and other protocol integrations
  • Ecosystem Rewards: Incentives, loyalty programs, airdrops, and participation rewards
  • Speculation: Market-priced asset for traders seeking exposure to independent DeFi tokens
  • Cross-Chain: Bridge between supported blockchains seamlessly

6. Governance & Transparency

Smart contract source code is publicly available on GitHub and can be independently reviewed. Major changes to tokenomics or core parameters are announced through official channels (Telegram, X, website).

Official addresses and documentation links are kept up to date in this repository and on explorers. We believe in radical transparency — no hidden functions, no admin keys that can freeze funds, no centralized control.

Community Governance: Future governance mechanisms may be implemented to allow token holders to vote on protocol upgrades and treasury allocations.
